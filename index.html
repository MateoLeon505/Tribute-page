Vincent van Gogh

One of the greatest representatives of post-impressionism in history

Here's a timeline of van Gogh's works of art:

  • 1885 - The Potato Eaters
  • 1888 - Sunflowers
  • 1888 - The Bedroom
  • 1888 - The Starry Night Over the Rhône
  • 1888 - Café Terrace at Night
  • 1889 - Starry Night
  • 1889 - Irises
  • 1989 - Self-Portrait with Bandaged Ear
  • 1990 - Portrait of Dr. Gachet
  • 1990 - Wheatfield with Crows

Contributions

Vincent van Gogh pioneered a bold artistic style, marked by vibrant colors and expressive brushwork. Despite facing limited recognition in his lifetime, his enduring legacy continues to inspire artists worldwide, making his works timeless objects of study and admiration.
